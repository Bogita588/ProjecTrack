Frequently Asked Questions

📌

What is ProjecTrack?

ProjecTrack is a citizen-driven platform that tracks government-funded projects in Kenya to promote transparency and accountability.

📊

Where does your project data come from?

We use publicly available datasets, investigative reports, and citizen submissions to build a crowdsourced database.

🗣️

Can I submit feedback on a project?

Yes! You can rate, comment, and highlight issues directly through the feedback section on each project page.
